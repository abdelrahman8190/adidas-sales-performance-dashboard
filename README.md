# Adidas US Interactive Sales Dashboard

![Dashboard Preview](Screenshot%202026-06-18%20212556.png)

## 📌 Project Overview
An interactive data analytics dashboard designed to track, visualize, and analyze Adidas sales performance, profit margins, and distribution channels. This project transforms raw transactional retail data into actionable business intelligence, tracking multi-year performance (2023–2025) across diverse sales methods and regional markets.

---
## 🚀 Key Features & Visualizations

* **KPI Tracking:** Real-time visibility into overall Units Sold, Total Profit ($85,070), and active market regions.
* **Profit Per Product:** Segmentation by category and gender (e.g., Footwear vs. Apparel for Men/Women) to isolate high-performing inventory.
* **Sales Method Breakdown:** A pie-chart analysis showcasing revenue split across Online (46%), Retail (35%), Outlet (12%), and Wholesale (7%) channels.
* **Geographical Insights:** Regional sales distribution donut chart paired with a dynamic **Geospatial Profit Map** covering key Middle Eastern markets (Egypt, Saudi Arabia, Iraq, Lebanon, Oman).
* **Time-Series Analysis:** A quarterly sales trend line chart spanning from Q1 2023 through Q3 2025 to evaluate seasonality and growth patterns.
* **Payment Method Analysis:** Comparative horizontal bar chart tracking profit generation across modern fintech solutions (Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, UPI) and traditional methods.
* **Interactive Slicers:** Full dynamic filtering capabilities by **Order Date**, **Region**, and **Payment Method** for tailored data deep-dives.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools Used
* **Microsoft Excel / Power BI:** Data modeling, DAX/formulas, and visualization architecture.
* **Power Query:** ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) pipeline for data cleansing and normalization.
* **Geospatial Mapping Engine:** Bing Maps integration for geographic profit distribution.

---

## 📈 Key Insights Derived
1. **Digital Dominance:** Online sales represent the largest single transactional channel at **46%**, indicating strong e-commerce health.
2. **Footwear is King:** Men's and Women's footwear categories represent the highest profit centers for the brand.
3. **Regional Leaders:** Egypt and Iraq lead the territorial market share, driving over **58%** of regional sales combined.
